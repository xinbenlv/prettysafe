# PrettySafe Design System

## 1. Brand Identity
* **Name:** PrettySafe
* **Core Vibe:** Organic Tech, Clean, Approachable, Secure but Soft.
* **Visual Motif:** Lotus, Water, Shield, Smooth Curves.

## 2. Color Palette

### Primary Colors (The "Safe" Green)
* **Primary Brand:** `#1CD17D` (Vibrant Mint)
    * *Usage:* Primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, active states, progress bars.
* **Dark Accent:** `#0F663C` (Deep Forest)
    * *Usage:* Headings, Logo outlines, strong borders, dark mode alternatives.

### Secondary Colors (The "Lotus" Pink)
* **Secondary Brand:** `#F0A3B0` (Soft Lotus Pink)
    * *Usage:* Secondary buttons, badges, "Success/Found" highlights, decorative blobs.

### Neutrals
* **Background:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) or `#F7FCF9` (Very Pale Mint Tint).
* **Text (Primary):** `#1A2F23` (Dark Jungle Green) — *Do not use pure black.*
* **Text (Secondary):** `#5C7A69` (Muted Sage) — *For descriptions/labels.*
* **Border/Divider:** `#E6F2EB` (Light Mint Grey).

## 3. Typography
* **Font Family:** `Outfit` (Preferred) or `Inter`.
* **Weight:**
    * Headings: SemiBold (600) or Bold (700).
    * Body: Regular (400) or Medium (500).

## 4. UI Component Styles

### Shapes & Radius
* **Global Radius:** `rounded-2xl` or `rounded-3xl` (Large, friendly curves).
* **Buttons:** `rounded-full` (Pill shape).

### Shadows (Soft & Colored)
* **Card Shadow:** `shadow-sm` or `shadow-md` with a slight green tint.
* **Glow Effect:** `box-shadow: 0 4px 14px 0 rgba(28, 209, 125, 0.39)` (For primary actions).

### Layout
* **Header:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) to blend with the Logo.
* **Body:** Very Light Mint (`#F7FCF9`) to create subtle separation.
